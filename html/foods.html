FOODS

Favorite foods of Rizal


Did you know that in addition to being a doctor, scholar, polyglot, poet, essayist, and novelist, the national hero of the Philippines José Rizal's also a foodie? According to popular historians and academicians, his love for food reflected on his writings-- in the journals that he kept, as well as the novels he wrote. Letters to his family ('Reminiscences and Travels of José Rizal' and 'One Hundred Letters of José Rizal to his Parents, Brothers, Sisters, Relatives') were also a good source of information because in them are requests for certain dishes.

Laguna cheese
Tinola
Sardinas secas (tuyo) with rice
Atis
Anchovies or Dilis
Ginisang Munggo
Hot Chocolate
Foie Gras
Suman
